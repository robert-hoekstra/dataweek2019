The Bird is the Word

Visualizing Sound and Breeding spots in Amsterdam

About this project

The Bird is the Word is a school project run by three student from the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. The team of students consist of two visual skilled students: Oemar and Andrick. And one technical student: Robert. The purpose of the project is to combine the two skills and make a data visualization of Amsterdam. The visualization should be appealing to the eye and more importantly, show new insights to the city with the use of several datasets.

You can check out the 'resources' page to see which datasets we used!
